Requirements: At this time only the Chrome browser is supported.

Tech: This is a Gameboy Camera emulator from Joey Dehnert made with HTML5 and javascript. It uses the getUserMedia() method to stream video from your computer's webcam then renders to a video element, which then gets drawn onto a corresponding canvas element where the 4 monochromatic gameboy color filters are applied. When a photo is taken the video redraw is stopped and the chosen frame is then processed and drawn onto a hidden canvas element and given a Gameboy frame and output to a base64 encoded image. When the images are shared the output image is automattically uploaded to IMGUR using their upload api. Example output image: http://i.imgur.com/0zNmjEv.png
